Workout Plans

Beginner Plan

This plan is perfect for newcomers to fitness. It focuses on low-impact exercises that build stamina and strength. Includes 3 workouts per week:

  • Day 1: Full-body workout (Bodyweight exercises)
  • Day 2: Cardio (Brisk walk or light jog for 30 minutes)
  • Day 3: Core Strength (Planks, crunches, leg raises)
View Plan

Intermediate Plan

This plan is designed for those who have been training for a while. It incorporates strength training and more complex exercises for muscle growth. 4-5 workouts per week:

  • Day 1: Upper body (Push-ups, dumbbell press, bicep curls)
  • Day 2: Lower body (Squats, lunges, deadlifts)
  • Day 3: Cardio (HIIT workouts)
  • Day 4: Full-body workout (Resistance bands and bodyweight exercises)
View Plan

Advanced Plan

Take your fitness to the next level with this intense plan. Focuses on strength, endurance, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT). 5-6 days per week:

  • Day 1: Powerlifting (Squats, deadlifts, bench press)
  • Day 2: HIIT (High-intensity interval training)
  • Day 3: Endurance training (Running or cycling for 45 minutes)
  • Day 4: Core and Abs (Russian twists, leg raises, flutter kicks)
  • Day 5: Active Recovery (Yoga or swimming)
